Chapter 1 "Basic Concepts in Reverse Engineering"
"The Problem"
The aim of reverse engineering is to increase productivity through improved documentation.

"What Is Reverse Engineering"
Reverse Engineering is a four-stage process [where the stages] consist of data evaluation, data generation, design verification, and design implementation.

Stage 1: evaluation and verification
Stage 2: technical data generation
Stage 3: design verification
Stage 4: project implementation
